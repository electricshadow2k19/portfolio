AI and ML Basics Quiz

Question 1: Image and Video Recognition

Q: What foundational model is specialized for image and video recognition tasks?

Options:

  • a) Convolutional Neural Networks
  • b) Large Language Models
  • c) Reinforcement Learning Models
  • d) Generative Adversarial Networks
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: CNNs are specifically designed for processing visual data and are the foundational model for image and video recognition tasks.

Question 2: AI in Finance

Q: In which domain is AI used for fraud detection and stock market prediction?

Options:

  • a) Finance
  • b) Retail
  • c) Healthcare
  • d) Transportation
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: The finance domain extensively uses AI for fraud detection, algorithmic trading, and stock market prediction.

Question 3: CNN Primary Role

Q: What is the primary role of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)?

Options:

  • a) To process and recognize images and videos
  • b) To manage databases
  • c) To analyze financial markets
  • d) To generate human-like text
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: CNNs are specifically designed to process and recognize patterns in visual data like images and videos.

Question 4: Ethical AI Considerations

Q: Which of the following ethical considerations focus on ensuring models are unbiased and fair?

Options:

  • a) Bias and Fairness
  • b) Privacy
  • c) Accountability
  • d) Transparency
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Bias and fairness specifically address the need to ensure AI models don't discriminate and treat all individuals fairly.

Question 5: Foundational Model Types

Q: Which of the following is NOT a type of foundational model?

Options:

  • a) Physical Models
  • b) Multimodal Models
  • c) Vision Models
  • d) Reinforcement Learning Models
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Physical models are not a type of AI foundational model. The other options are all valid foundational model types.

Question 6: Deep Learning Applications

Q: What are some common applications of Deep Learning in everyday life?

Options:

  • a) Simple calculators
  • b) Basic text editors
  • c) Image recognition on social media, voice assistants like Siri and Alexa
  • d) Weather forecasting
Correct Answer: C

Explanation: Deep learning is commonly used in image recognition on social media platforms and voice assistants like Siri and Alexa.

Question 7: Multimodal Models

Q: Which type of AI model can process and generate more than one type of data?

Options:

  • a) Vision Models
  • b) Supervised Learning Models
  • c) Multimodal Models
  • d) Reinforcement Learning Models
Correct Answer: C

Explanation: Multimodal models can process and generate multiple types of data (text, images, audio, etc.).

Question 8: Large Language Models

Q: What is an example of a Large Language Model (LLM)?

Options:

  • a) VGGNet
  • b) ResNet
  • c) EfficientNet
  • d) GPT-3
Correct Answer: D

Explanation: GPT-3 is a well-known example of a Large Language Model, while the others are computer vision models.

Question 9: Artificial Intelligence Definition

Q: What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Options:

  • a) A software used for video editing
  • b) A type of computer hardware
  • c) A program that can play chess
  • d) Machines designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence
Correct Answer: D

Explanation: AI refers to machines designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Question 10: Reinforcement Learning

Q: Which type of learning involves rewards and penalties?

Options:

  • a) Supervised Learning
  • b) Unsupervised Learning
  • c) Reinforcement Learning
  • d) Semi-supervised Learning
Correct Answer: C

Explanation: Reinforcement learning uses a reward and penalty system to train models.

Question 11: AI in Gaming and Robotics

Q: What type of AI model is often used in gaming, robotics, and strategic planning?

Options:

  • a) Vision Models
  • b) Reinforcement Learning Models
  • c) Generative Models
  • d) Multimodal Models
Correct Answer: B

Explanation: Reinforcement learning models are commonly used in gaming, robotics, and strategic planning scenarios.

Question 12: Machine Learning Definition

Q: Which of the following is a subset of AI focused on learning from data?

Options:

  • a) Data Analysis
  • b) Neural Networks
  • c) Machine Learning
  • d) Deep Learning
Correct Answer: C

Explanation: Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on learning patterns from data.

Question 13: AI Popularity Factors

Q: Which of the following is a significant reason for the recent explosion in AI popularity?

Options:

  • a) Reduction in data availability
  • b) Decline in algorithmic improvements
  • c) Lack of investment
  • d) Advances in GPU and cloud computing
Correct Answer: D

Explanation: Advances in GPU technology and cloud computing have been major drivers of AI's recent popularity.

Question 14: Alan Turing's Contribution

Q: Alan Turing is known for which significant contribution to AI?

Options:

  • a) Developing the "Turing Test"
  • b) Inventing the first computer
  • c) Developing neural networks
  • d) Coining the term "Artificial Intelligence"
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Alan Turing developed the "Turing Test" as a measure of machine intelligence.

Question 15: AI in Healthcare

Q: Name an application of AI in healthcare.

Options:

  • a) Email marketing
  • b) Predictive diagnostics
  • c) Social media management
  • d) Content creation
Correct Answer: B

Explanation: Predictive diagnostics is a key application of AI in healthcare, helping to predict diseases and conditions.
