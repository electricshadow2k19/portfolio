Question 1: Image and Video Recognition

Q: What foundational model is specialized for image and video recognition tasks?

Options:

  • a) Convolutional Neural Networks
  • b) Large Language Models
  • c) Reinforcement Learning Models
  • d) Generative Adversarial Networks
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: CNNs are specifically designed for processing visual data and are the foundational model for image and video recognition tasks.

Question 2: AI in Finance

Q: In which domain is AI used for fraud detection and stock market prediction?

Options:

  • a) Finance
  • b) Retail
  • c) Healthcare
  • d) Transportation
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: The finance domain extensively uses AI for fraud detection, algorithmic trading, and stock market prediction.

Question 3: CNN Primary Role

Q: What is the primary role of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)?

Options:

  • a) To process and recognize images and videos
  • b) To manage databases
  • c) To analyze financial markets
  • d) To generate human-like text
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: CNNs are specifically designed to process and recognize patterns in visual data like images and videos.

Question 4: Ethical AI Considerations

Q: Which of the following ethical considerations focus on ensuring models are unbiased and fair?

Options:

  • a) Bias and Fairness
  • b) Privacy
  • c) Accountability
  • d) Transparency
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Bias and fairness specifically address the need to ensure AI models don't discriminate and treat all individuals fairly.

Question 5: Foundational Model Types

Q: Which of the following is NOT a type of foundational model?

Options:

  • a) Physical Models
  • b) Multimodal Models
  • c) Vision Models
  • d) Reinforcement Learning Models
Correct Answer: A

Explanation: Physical models are not a type of AI foundational model. The other options are all valid foundational model types.
